This implementation allows admins to define and assign user roles. The `Role` and `UserRole` models handle role definitions and user-role assignments, respectively. The `RoleService` provides the necessary business logic, while the controller and routes handle the API endpoints. The `auth_decorator` ensures only admins can access role management functionalities.
